Case Study.

JD PokeMuse — From eBay seller
to a full digital presence.

The challenge.

Gary runs one of Australia's biggest Japanese Pokémon TCG stores on eBay, with customers worldwide. But eBay alone has limits. There was no dedicated website, no place to tell the brand story, no digital home that matched the scale of what he had built. He needed something professional, fast, and built from the ground up.

The approach.

Starting from scratch, I designed and built a fully custom website that functions as both a brand introduction and a funnel. Every page was built with intent: a homepage that communicates trust instantly, a product overview that covers everything a customer wants to know, and a direct connection to Gary's eBay store. No templates, no shortcuts.

The delivery.

The complete site was designed, built, and delivered within a week. Gary had a live, professional website that matched the reputation of his business from day one.

1 Week to deliver
100% Custom built
0 Templates used
